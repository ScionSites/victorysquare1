VST: Our Story

We were founded to fill the early-stage investment gap.

Victory Square is a venture builder building the next tech giants. Our sweet spot is the cutting-edge tech shaping the 4th Industrial Revolution. Our portfolio consists of 25+ global next-generation tech companies across the Creator Economy, Digital Health, Gaming, Web 3.0, VR/AR and Climate Tech.

We provide VC investment access to the next tech giants in an audited, transparent and liquid vehicle — effectively democratizing access to the next generation of the best startups.

Unparalleled access to startups

Through our internal incubator, Living Labs, and accelerators like Launch Academy — with more than 80 global accelerator partners.

Hands-on from incubation to monetization

Our management team and advisors are actively involved, providing financial, operational and strategic support to scale globally.

Aligned with investors

Management holds 20%+ ownership of the funds' assets. We drive value by monetizing investments and reinvesting the gains.

Responsibility

ESG woven through everything we do.

Victory Square integrates a strong environmental, social and corporate governance component throughout its operations. Our portfolio highlights minority entrepreneurs often overlooked by traditional investors, including many from developing countries.

We're dedicated to giving back through our time, talent and treasure — supporting organizations serving youth, mental health, special needs, sport, tech, education, marginalized groups, First Nations and accessibility.
