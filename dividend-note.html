Dividend Note for Canadian Shareholders

From time to time, Victory Square Technologies Inc. ("VST") declares special common share dividends representing an interest in shares of its portfolio companies. This page provides general information for Canadian-resident shareholders. It is not tax advice — please consult your own professional advisor regarding your specific circumstances.

Eligible dividend designation

Unless otherwise indicated, dividends paid by Victory Square Technologies Inc. to Canadian residents are designated as "eligible dividends" for the purposes of the Income Tax Act (Canada) and any corresponding provincial or territorial tax legislation.

Special common share dividends

Where VST distributes shares of a portfolio company as a dividend-in-kind, registered and beneficial shareholders of record on the applicable record date receive their pro-rata entitlement in accordance with the terms of each declaration. Details of record dates, payable dates and distribution ratios are set out in the corresponding news release.

Where to find filings

Official declarations, financial statements and continuous disclosure documents are filed on Canada's securities filing system. Please refer to SEDAR+ and our News & Media section for the latest announcements.
