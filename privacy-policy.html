Privacy Policy

Subject to British Columbia's Personal Information Protection Act.

Victory Square Technologies ("Victory Square Technologies" or "VST" or "We" or "Us") respects the privacy of your personal information and is committed to protecting it and using it solely for the purpose for which it was collected. The objective of this privacy policy is to inform you of our privacy practices — why and how VST collects your personal information, and how it is kept and used.

Scope and application

This policy applies to all personal information collected by Victory Square Technologies from the website www.victorysquare.com (the "Website") and also through communications you may have directly with us by e-mail, phone or otherwise. This policy is subject to British Columbia's Personal Information Protection Act, as well as other applicable legislation and regulations.

What we collect and why

We collect certain types of personal information from users communicating with us — in particular when such persons request information through the Website regarding Victory Square Technologies or our portfolio companies, or subscribe to a mailing list. In most cases, the personal information requested is: name, business name, e-mail address and phone number.

This information is collected and used notably for the following reasons:

  • To answer your request for information and to ensure follow-up with you regarding your request;
  • To send out communications regarding our portfolio companies;
  • To improve the content of our website.

The personal information we collect is used solely for the purpose for which it was collected. You can choose to receive communications from us by filing the form on our Website and/or by providing your express consent, and you may withdraw consent or unsubscribe at any time.

Electronic information on our Website

We may sometimes collect information that is not of a personal nature, such as the date and time of your request, the type of Internet browser or operating system you use, or the domain name from which you were linked to our Website. If you use a mobile device, we may also collect information about your device, such as device ID and type, as well as usage information. This information will not be linked to the personal information you provide when you sign up to receive communications.

How your information is stored and communicated

Victory Square Technologies keeps personal information for the period of time it deems reasonable to fulfill the identified purposes or in accordance with the law, whichever is longer. Once the identified purposes have been reached and your information is no longer required — or if you request that it be deleted — we ensure it remains anonymous or destroy it in a way that protects against unauthorized access.

We never disclose your personal information to anyone outside of Victory Square Technologies without your consent, except for the following:

  • Our goods or services providers who carry out duties on our behalf (e.g. data processing, media), under contractual protections;
  • Any entity resulting from a merger or change in control of VST, limited to the objectives of that transaction, or any public authority where the law requires;
  • Our portfolio companies, under the same contractual protections;
  • We do not transfer your personal information to a promotional partner unless you have specifically given us consent.

How do I remove my information?

We want to communicate with you only if you want to hear from us. If you prefer not to receive information from us — by email, postal mail or phone — please follow the unsubscribe instructions within the email message or contact us at info@victorysquare.com. Such requests may take up to two weeks to become effective.

Security

All data is stored on high-security servers, protected against misuse and access by unauthorized individuals. This information is only made available to individuals (employees or agents) who require access to carry out their responsibilities.

External hyperlinks

Our Website may include hyperlinks to independent websites. By clicking such hyperlinks, you leave our Website. VST does not control the operation of these websites, and any personal information you submit through them is subject to their own privacy policies.

Changes to this policy

From time to time, Victory Square Technologies may modify its privacy policy. Please consult it periodically. In the event of a modification, we will update the date of the document.

Information & contact

Should you have any questions regarding our privacy policy, access to personal information, the collection, use and disclosure of personal information, how to file a complaint, or if you wish to withdraw your name from our mailing lists or update your information, please contact us at info@victorysquare.com or call 604-283-9166.
